How LLM pricing actually works

Almost every provider charges per token, split into input and output rates, quoted per 1 million tokens. Output is usually 2‚Äì5√ó more expensive than input, so chatty models that generate long answers cost far more than the headline input price suggests. The formula:

How to cut your AI bill

  • Right-size the model. Small models (mini/flash/haiku tiers) are often 10‚Äì30√ó cheaper and good enough for classification, extraction and simple chat.
  • Trim your prompts. Long system prompts and stuffed context get billed on every call. Cache or summarize.
  • Cap output length. Set max tokens. Output is where the money goes.
  • Use prompt caching where supported to cut repeated-context cost dramatically.
  • Batch non-urgent jobs ‚Äî several providers offer ~50% off batch processing.
